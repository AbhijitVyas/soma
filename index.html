The Socio-physical Model of Activities (SOMA) is a formal activity model for embodied agents that need to operate their body to generate motions that cause intentional effects in the physical and social world.

Credit goes to Ontology Task Force for generating such an innovative approach. Please take a look at first soma version online on webvowl webbased ontology viewer. You can feel free to browse through different SOMA branches and can also search specific concept.
